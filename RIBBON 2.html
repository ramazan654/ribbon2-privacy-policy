Privacy Policy for RIBBON 2

RIBBON 2 does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data from users.

The app uses Bluetooth and Core MIDI technologies solely to connect to external MIDI devices (such as MIDI controllers or keyboards) for musical purposes. No personal information, location data, or usage analytics are collected, stored, or transmitted.

All MIDI and Bluetooth communication occurs locally on the user's device and is not sent to any external servers.

RIBBON 2 does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
